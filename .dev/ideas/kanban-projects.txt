Kanban Project Thingy

Problem: You need a personal project board that respects your focus limits and doesn't encourage multitasking burnout.

Dream: Projects have identity (icon, color, emoji). Tasks move through states (to-do, in progress, done, archived). The board intelligently limits how many tasks can be "in progress" at once. Archive instead of delete. Subtasks, due dates, notes per task. One-click compact PDF export of a whole project. Feels calm, not corporate.

MVP: A single board with three columns, a WIP limit per column, basic task cards with due dates and subtasks, and an archive section. No PDF yet.
