Hondana is a personal, private library manager designed to unify tracking for all your content—anime, books, courses, podcasts, and more. It solves the problem of scattered progress tracking and notes by providing a single system where you can manage everything you consume. For each content item (with a title, type, cover art, and total length), you can create multiple intention tracks (such as Fun, Learning, or Immersion), each with its own independent status (Plan to Start, In Progress, Completed, On Hold, Dropped), progress counter, goals with due dates, and associated notes, quotes, or actionables. The app also includes a separate recommendation inbox for future content, privacy controls to hide sensitive items, gentle nudges to prevent over-consuming one genre, and a people directory for authors and creators.
